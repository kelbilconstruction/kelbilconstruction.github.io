About Us

Building Excellence.
Engineering the Future.

Kelbil Construction and Supplies Ltd is a dynamic, multi-disciplinary engineering and construction company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We were established with a clear mission: to deliver world-class engineering consultancy, construction supervision, and supply chain solutions across East Africa.

Our integrated service model — spanning design, supervision, environmental assessment, surveying, solar energy, CCTV, and materials supply — positions Kelbil as a one-stop partner for complex infrastructure and building projects of all scales, serving individuals, private developers, corporates, government agencies, parastatals, and NGOs.

Kelbil brings together a team of EBK-registered engineers, architects, environmental scientists, and project managers delivering innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective solutions to clients in both the public and private sectors.
